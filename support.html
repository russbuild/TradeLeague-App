Getting Started

TradeLeague lets you compete with friends in real-time trading competitions. To get started, create an account, join or create a league, and link your brokerage account through Plaid to start tracking your performance.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create or join a league?

From the home screen, tap the "+" button to create a new league. To join an existing league, ask the league creator for an invite link or code.

How do I link my brokerage account?

Go to your profile settings and tap "Link Brokerage Account." TradeLeague uses Plaid to securely connect to your broker. We never store your login credentials.

Is my financial data safe?

Yes. TradeLeague uses Plaid, a trusted financial data platform, to connect to your brokerage. Your login credentials are never stored on our servers. We only access portfolio and transaction data necessary to power the competitions.

How do I manage my subscription?

Premium subscriptions are managed through Apple. To cancel or modify your subscription, go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone.

How do I delete my account?

To request account deletion, please email us at the address below. We will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days of your request.

Troubleshooting

  • App crashing or freezing: Make sure you're running the latest version of TradeLeague and that your iPhone software is up to date.
  • Brokerage not syncing: Try unlinking and relinking your account through Plaid in your profile settings.
  • Leaderboard not updating: Portfolio data refreshes periodically. Pull down on the leaderboard screen to force a refresh.
