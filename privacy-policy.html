Privacy Policy for TradeLeague

Last Updated: February 19, 2026

PHVZED, LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the TradeLeague mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using TradeLeague, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, username, and profile information.
  • Authentication Data: We use Firebase Authentication to manage your account. If you sign in using a third-party provider (e.g., Apple or Google), we may receive basic profile information from that provider.
  • Financial Account Information: If you choose to link a brokerage account through Plaid, Plaid collects and transmits your financial data (such as account holdings and transaction history) to our App. We do not store your brokerage login credentials. Please refer to Plaid's Privacy Policy for more information.
  • User Content: Posts, trades, comments, and other content you share within the App.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the App, including features used, pages viewed, and actions taken.
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, and crash reports.

Information from Third-Party Services

  • Plaid: Portfolio and transaction data from linked brokerage accounts.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription and purchase status.
  • Firebase: Analytics, authentication, and cloud storage data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Facilitate trading competitions and leaderboards
  • Display portfolio performance and trade activity
  • Process subscriptions and in-app purchases
  • Improve, personalize, and optimize the App
  • Communicate with you about updates, features, and support
  • Monitor and analyze usage trends
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues or fraud

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Your username, profile information, trades, and competition results may be visible to other TradeLeague users as part of the App's social features.
  • Service Providers: We share data with third-party services that help us operate the App, including Firebase (backend and authentication), Plaid (financial data), and RevenueCat (subscription management).
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests by public authorities.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and Update: You can access and update your account information through the App's settings.
  • Delete Your Account: You can request account deletion by contacting us at the email address below.
  • Unlink Financial Accounts: You can disconnect your brokerage account from Plaid at any time within the App.
  • Push Notifications: You can opt out of push notifications through your device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

TradeLeague is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

8. Third-Party Links and Services

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
